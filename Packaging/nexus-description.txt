[size=5][b]Skip Intro[/b][/size]

A BepInEx mod for Everything is Crab. Skips the two splash videos at game startup. Goes straight to the main menu.

[size=4][b]Requirements[/b][/size]

[list]
[*]Everything is Crab (tested 1.0.1__8213)
[*]BepInEx 6 BleedingEdge IL2CPP (CoreCLR), build 755 or newer
[/list]

The stable BepInEx 5 / 6 packs do not work. Unity 6 IL2CPP needs the BleedingEdge build.

[size=4][b]Install: easiest way[/b][/size]

Use [url=https://www.nexusmods.com/everythingiscrab/mods/2]Crab Mod Manager[/url] (or [url=https://github.com/rinpoche-peregrine/CrabModManager]grab it from GitHub[/url]).

[list=1]
[*]Place CrabModManager.exe in your Everything is Crab install folder.
[*]Run it. Click "Install BepInEx" if you do not have BepInEx yet.
[*]Launch the game once, then close it (this generates BepInEx interop files).
[*]Download this Skip Intro zip from Nexus.
[*]Drag the zip onto the Crab Mod Manager window.
[*]Launch the game.
[/list]

[size=4][b]Install: manual[/b][/size]

[list=1]
[*]Install BepInEx 6 BE 755+ from [url=https://builds.bepinex.dev/projects/bepinex_be]builds.bepinex.dev[/url]. winhttp.dll should sit next to Everything is Crab.exe.
[*]Launch the game once to generate BepInEx/interop/.
[*]Download this Skip Intro zip from Nexus.
[*]Extract plugins/Bungus-SkipIntro/ into your BepInEx/plugins/ folder. Final path: BepInEx/plugins/Bungus-SkipIntro/SkipIntroPlugin.dll
[*]Launch the game.
[/list]

[size=4][b]Uninstall[/b][/size]

Click Uninstall in Crab Mod Manager, or delete the Bungus-SkipIntro folder from BepInEx/plugins/.
